What is Vulcan?

We are an award-winning community robotics team composed of ten high school students from the Bay Area. September 2017 will mark our fourth year participating in the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC), an annual competition where students from around the world build robots to complete game challenges. For more information on FIRST and the FTC competition, visit the official FIRST website.

What is FIRST?

FIRST is an organization which runs robotics programs for minors of all age levels. It has a presence throughout the world and is responsible for FIRST Lego League Jr., FIRST Lego League, FIRST Tech Challenge, and First Robotics Competition.

What is FTC?

Vulcan Robotics Team participates in FTC. FTC, or FIRST Tech Challenge, is a robotics competition run by FIRST and is designed for middle and high school students.
